Quick Pasta Recipes for Busy Weeknights

These three recipes can all be made in under 20 minutes.

1. Garlic Butter Linguine

Cook linguine. Melt 3 tbsp butter with 4 cloves minced garlic. Toss with pasta, parmesan, and fresh parsley.

2. One-Pot Tomato Basil Penne

Add penne, crushed tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and basil to one pot. Boil until pasta is cooked. Top with mozzarella.

3. Lemon Cream Spaghetti

Cook spaghetti. Mix heavy cream, lemon zest, lemon juice, and parmesan. Toss with hot pasta.
